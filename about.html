Hey there!

I'm a product and brand designer whose work focuses on creating user experiences that help move companies forward.

Since founding a beauty/tech venture, POSH, I've been passionate about creating meaningful experiences for people who make things. I've helped run and build sustained, creator communities like Startup Shell, a student-run incubator and co-working space at the University of Maryland, as well as Terp Designers, an online and in-person community of student designers. I've also created and co-instructed a for-credit course at UMD called Digital Product Design (IDEA258Z). I graduated (B.S. Biology, December 2018) and currently I'm designing at Facebook.

Aside from design, I love talking about community cultures, indoor gardening and sci-fi stuff. Feel free to get in touch if you'd like to chat or work together!
